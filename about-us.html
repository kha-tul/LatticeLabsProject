We are building a highly scalable blockchain ecosystem

At Lattice Network, we’re committed to providing technology that combines scalability, decentralization, and security. To turn this vision into reality, the Lattice Network is developing different parts of an open system and closely works with partners to support the adoption of Lattice Network technology.

We are blockchain
developers

The Lattice Network has experience with blockchain development across the whole technology stack. From developing a consensus algorithm from scratch to middleware and a fullfeatured software suite, we built it.

We are blockchain
integrators

Through the EVM, developers can utilize smart contracts on top of Lattice Network, and it allows projects to port their existing Ethereum dApps over to Lattice Network easily.

Additionally, Lachesis is ABCI compatible and can be seamlessly integrated into other blockchains as a separate consensus module.

About us

We are a decentralized global company comprised of blockchain experts, entrepreneurs, engineers, computer scientists, designers, consultants, government officials, and business leaders across 4 continents. Meet the team building the future of Web 3.0.
